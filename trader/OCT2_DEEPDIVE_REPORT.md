## Critical Conclusions

### 1. The Stop Placement Problem

**Current Strategy**: Stop at exact resistance level
- **Pros**: Maintains PS60 discipline, tight risk control
- **Cons**: No room for normal price noise, whipsaws on volatile opens

**Evidence**:
- Trades with 0-0.04% risk got stopped immediately
- LCID & INTC both rallied 1.5-4.9% after stops
- Both had RIGHT conditions (up trend, volume) but WRONG entry timing

### 2. Market Open Volatility Issue

**All trades entered 09:30-10:40**:
- First 5-10 minutes = highest volatility
- Spreads wider, noise higher
- Many trades stopped in <1 minute

**Solution**: Wait for 09:45-10:00 for volatility to settle?

### 3. The INTC Case Study - What We Learned

INTC was a **PERFECT SETUP that we COMPLETELY MISSED**:
- ✅ Scanner correct (resistance $35.79)
- ✅ Breakout confirmed (hit $37.57, +4.97%)
- ✅ Target1 hit ($37.53 at 3:28 PM)
- ❌ We stopped out at $35.79 immediately
- ❌ Lost entire 4.9% move due to 0% risk entry

**Lesson**: Even low-score stocks (70) can work if the breakout is real. We need to give them room to breathe.

### 4. Scanner Accuracy Assessment

**Scanner was RIGHT on 8/8 stocks**:
- All resistance levels tested
- 1 stock (INTC) hit Target1
- Levels were accurate

**Problem was NOT the scanner, it was**:
- Entry timing (market open volatility)
- Stop placement (too tight at exact resistance)
- No buffer for normal retracement

### Immediate Changes to Test

1. **Add Stop Buffer** (0.3-0.5% below resistance)
   - LCID: Stop $24.41 instead of $24.53 → Would have held
   - INTC: Stop $35.61 instead of $35.79 → Would have held

2. **Delay Entry** (wait 15-30 min after open)
   - Avoid 09:30-09:45 volatility

3. **Require Volume Confirmation**
   - INTC had SURGING volume (correct)
   - ROKU had DECLINING volume (failed)
   - Use volume as entry filter

4. **Minimum Risk Threshold**
   - Don't accept 0-0.04% risk trades
   - Require minimum 0.3% risk (room for noise)

### Strategic Questions

1. **Is PS60 discipline too strict for automated trading?**
   - Manual traders can adjust

2. **Should we accept slightly larger losses for fewer whipsaws?**
   - Current: Many small losses (<$100)
   - Alternative: Fewer but larger losses (0.3-0.5% buffer)

3. **Can we filter by market conditions?**
