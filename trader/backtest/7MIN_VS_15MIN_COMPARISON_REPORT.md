## Root Cause Analysis: Why 15-Min Rule Failed

### 1. **Bad Setups Stay Bad**
- If a trade goes negative in 7 minutes, it RARELY recovers by 15 minutes
- Market already "voted" on the setup - extended time just bleeds more

### 2. **Market Momentum is Established Quickly**
- Real breakouts move immediately (within 3-5 minutes)
- If no movement after 7 minutes ‚Üí likely a failed breakout
- Waiting another 8 minutes doesn't change the underlying failure

### 3. **False Breakouts Reverse Quickly**
- False breakouts show their true nature in first 5-7 minutes
- Extended holding catches the FULL reversal move against you
- Better to exit early and preserve capital

### 4. **October 8 Market Conditions**
- Choppy, whipsaw market environment
- NO amount of extended time would have helped
- Problem was SETUP QUALITY, not timeout duration
